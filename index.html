Use Technology Properly

Be Smart. Be Responsible.

Technology is powerful. It can help us learn, communicate, and grow. But it must be used wisely and responsibly.

  • ✔ Protect your personal information
  • ✔ Avoid spreading fake news
  • ✔ Use technology for learning
  • ✔ Balance screen time with real life
